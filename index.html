From First Call to Handover

How a job actually runs

  1. 01

    Inspect & Test

    We assess slab moisture, existing coatings and traffic load before recommending a system.

  2. 02

    Grind & Prep

    Diamond grinding strips contaminants and opens the pores of the slab for proper bond.

  3. 03

    Repair & Level

    Cracks, joints and low spots are filled and levelled so the finish reads as one continuous slab.

  4. 04

    Apply & Cure

    Densifier, resin or flake system goes down in a controlled, staged pour to hit cure windows.

  5. 05

    Inspect & Handover

    Final walk-through against spec, warranty paperwork issued, site handed back on schedule.
